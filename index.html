Exhibit F — Bootcamp Capstone

DSAI_ML — Green Hydrogen Fault Predictor

Clustering-based fault detection for alkaline electrolyzers

Ensemble ML

A fault-detection system for alkaline electrolyzers used in green hydrogen production. Unsupervised clustering groups sensor readings into normal and fault states without needing pre-labeled data, which then feeds an ensemble of Random Forest, Extra Trees, and Gradient Boosting models for the final classification.
